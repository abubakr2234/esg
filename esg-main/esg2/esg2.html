OUR PHILOSOPHY

South Suez recognises the importance of sustainable investing and is committed to promoting sound ESG management practices
within its portfolio. Thoughtful and well-implemented ESG management can enhance profitability and create significant value for
investors and stakeholders.

We have therefor developed a comprehensive policy on responsible investing that addresses identifcation, analysis and management
of potential ESG risks and opportunities, both before investment and post-investment. Addionally, the appropriate disclosure of all
ESG consideration is required by the underlying funds in which South Suez invests.
