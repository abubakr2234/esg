SIGNATORY OF UNPRI

The Principles for Responsible Investment reflects the increasing
relevance of environmental, social and corporate governance issues to
investment practices. South Suez will only invest with fund managers that
have ESG policies that are in line with the key standards set by the UNPRI. In signing the Principles, we as investors publicly commit to adopt and
implement them, where consistent with our fiduciary responsibilities.
We also evaluate the effectiveness and improve the content of the Principles
over time. Aiming to improve our ability to meet commitments to
beneficiaries as well as better align our investment activities with the
broader interests of society.
