OUR ESG FUND RATING
We have integrated an ESG Management System into our bespoke Risk Operating System. This allows us to adopt a methodological
approach to manage environmental & social risks and impacts in a structured way on an ongoing basis. An ESG rating is assigned to all the funds within our portfolio. The scorecard is based on the following criteria:
1
Portfolio Constituents
Are the fund managers’ investments deemed risky from an ESG perspective?
2
LPA Provision
Do the fund formation documents contain provisions for ESG reporting, and has the fund manager complied with those provisions?
3
Economic Sustainability
Are the funds’ portfolio companies performing well so that they can sustain employment and pay taxes?
4
Responsible Investment Principles
Does the fund subscribe to sustainability principles such as the UNPRI, IFC standards, etc.?
5
Resources
Does the fund manager have sufficient resources (staff, ESG system) to execute its ESG policy?
6
Charity
Does the fund manager have a charitable programme, which makes donations regularly?
